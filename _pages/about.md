I am an Analyst for Precision Insights at their New York City office. I leverage data analysis tools and techniques (SQL, Python), statistics, and project management to help tackle business problems in the B2B sector.

In regards to my education, I am a recent graduate from Brown University School of Engineering's Program in Innovation and Entrepreneurship (PRIME). I am also a Brown University undergraduate from the class of 2024, with a Bachelor of Arts in Business Economics and Political Science. Through my studies, I learned how to focus on the intersection of engineering, economics and politics to make more informed decisions.

Before Precision, my most recent work experience includes my full-time work at Chimera Securities, where I worked as an SIE and Series 57-licensed Equity Trader, and my internship at Cherrystone Angel Group, where I worked as a Marketing and Business Analyst. 

I believe that my diverse background has given me not just a more fulfilling life, but also a better understanding of how the world works. Ranging from business and politics, STEM and entrepreneurship, and music and foreign languages, my experiences allow me to think critically and solve any problem in any situation. Most importantly, I learned the importance of integrity, honesty, teamwork, and understanding each other.
